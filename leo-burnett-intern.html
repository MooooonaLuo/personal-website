LEO BURNETT INTERN

Visual Design Intern · 2018

OVERVIEW

In summar of 2018, I was glad to join the Audi Creative Team of Leo Burnett Beijing Communications Co., LTd., as a visual design intern. For two months, I worked on the advertising campaign for Audi Q2's publication with designers, copywriters, and innovation stratgists.

MY ROLE

Created graphic design for advertisement proposals. Designed the layout for an Audi handbook. Ideated for advertising strategies with the team

TAKEAWAY

I learned a lot about branding strategies and visual design skills. At first, I was shocked by the high cost of advertisements at first â€” even a single photo could cost million RMB. During the internship, I gradually recognized the power of advertisements. Successful advertisements are vital in building strong brand identities and creating unbreakable emotional bridge between brands and people. A brand that eventually stands out from thousands of competitors is the one that people are mentally connected with.

I learned and practiced visual design skills with Adobe Photoshop from my experienced co-worker everyday. With sophisticated skills and abundant experience in visual design, they were able to create a delicate poster design within a couple hours. I understand that to achieve their level, it takes years and years of efforts. Motivated by a desire to turn concepts into reality, I have developed a hobby to learn from excellent design works and practice visual design everyday.

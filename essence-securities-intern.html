ESSENCE SECURITIES INTERN

UI Design Intern · 2021

OVERVIEW

Established in 2006, Essence Securities Co., Ltd. is one of the leading securities corporations in China. In the summer of 2021, I worked at the Information and Technology Department in the company for three months as a UI Design Intern. In a small design team, I had the opportunities to take part in various kinds of projects, including but not limited to App design, mascot design, big-screen design, and poster design. Cooperating with designers, engineers, and product managers, I created graphics for company events and improved the existing company systems to increase productivity at work.

MY ROLE

Designed the user interface of Essence Asset Management mini APP. Collaborated with other designers to create department mascots. Designed icons, banners, and posters. Provided feedback for big-screen design

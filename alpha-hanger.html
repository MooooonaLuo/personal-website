ALPHA HANGER

Freelance Web Design · 2021

OVERVIEW

Alpha Hanger is a dry-cleaning equipment manufacturer located in Cambodia. It has a large client base in North America, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East and is among the top 3 companies in the industry. I designed the official website for the company to tighten their connection with their clients and introduce their service and technology to the world.

Live at www.hangeralpha.com

MY ROLE

Compiled creative brief that included website navigation, the illustration style, Moodboard, and problems anticipated. Turned photos shoot with phones into higher-quality images that could be used on the website. Assisted the computer engineer in the final delivery

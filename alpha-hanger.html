ALPHA HANGER

Freelance Web Design · 2021

OVERVIEW

Alpha Hanger is a dry-cleaning equipment manufacturer located in Cambodia. It has a large client base in North America, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East and is among the top 3 companies in the industry. I designed the official website for the company to tighten their connection with their clients and introduce their service and technology to the world.

Live at www.hangeralpha.com

MY ROLE

Compiled creative brief that included website navigation, the illustration style, Moodboard, and problems anticipated. Turned photos shoot with phones into higher-quality images that could be used on the website. Assisted the computer engineer in the final delivery

UNDERSTAND THE USERS

  • The website's system model needs to be easily understood by the elderly population, while the visuals can be innovative.

  • Texts need to be readable for the elderly population.

  • Pay attention to color contrast.

CHARACTERISTICS & STYLES

Together with my client, we identified five brand characteristics: trustworthy, friendly, vigorous, high-quality, and large-scale . Based on the characteristics and existing branding, I determined the colors and fonts for the website. I extracted the primary and secondary colors from the company's logo and slightly altered the hue and saturation. I chose Montserrat for titles since this font family delivers a sense of being reliable and professional.

MOOD BOARD & IMAGE STYLE GUIDES

The photo collage serves as a guide for image selection on the website. It demonstrates the moods delivered by images, image content, and image quality.

There is always a gap between expectation and reality. When discussing this part with my client, they informed me that they could not provide me with high-quality images due to a lack of professional devices and photographers. To overcome this issue, I guided the factory workers in Cambodia to shoot the best photos with what they had. I suggested they use the latest version of the iPhone and wrote detailed instructions on photography composition. The result turned out to be satisfactory.

WIREFRAME

I presented my client with a wireframe to collect feedback on website structure, information hierarchy, and navigation.

HOMEPAGE DESIGN

The homepage is crucial in delivering the right messages to users at the first glimpse. I proposed three homepage designs to my client. I personally preferred the first two options, but unexpectedly, my client considered the last one the best. To better understand their thoughts, I asked them to list the pros and cons of each design. I later realized that they have a logic behind their choice that I did not discover earlier. There are three things they cared about the most: displaying the images of their factory and products, having a unique style, and being perceived as reliable and professional. Based on the synthesis, I designed an optimized version that met their needs and was eventually adopted.

FINAL DELIVERY

I created a high-fidelity prototype and delivered it to the developer. I assisted him during the developing process to provide everything he needed and ensure the website matched my design.

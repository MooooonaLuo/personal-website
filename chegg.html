CHEGG INTERNSHIP

UX Design · 2022

OVERVIEW

The past summer, I interned as a UX designer at Chegg's Engagement UX team. I took ownership of the two projects I worked on. In my capstone project, I worked in a cross-functional team to improve the discoverability and engagement of Chegg's Course Dashboard. Due to the NDAs, I'm not able to share any details at this moment. Another project I worked on was building the first content design system on Figma with the content design team.

MY CONTRIBUTIONS

  • Led the design of two new features to increase the discoverability and engagement on Chegg Course Dashboard.
  • Partnered with the UX researcher to create the research plan and iterated design based on user testing findings.
  • Proactively presented my work in progress to collect feedback from stakeholders, designers, and accessibility expert.
  • Built the content design system with the content design team.

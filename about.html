WORK EXPERIMENTS ABOUT

Hi! My name is Jiayu Luo.
You can call me Mona.

I am an interaction designer based in NYC. Coming from a background in new media arts and UI/UX design, I have both an artist’s sensibility and a designer’s rationality.

I studied in the Interdisciplinary Computing and The Arts program at the University of California, San Diego, during my undergraduate years. This experience empowered me to have an artistic and humane perspective on human-computer interaction. With a desire to dive deeper into interaction design, in September 2021, I embarked on a new journey studying in the MFA Interaction Design program at the School of Visual Arts.

I perceive the world as an interactive platform and am interested in tackling problems beyond digital screens.

WORK EXPERIMENTS ABOUT

Hi! I'm Mona Luo.

I am a product designer passionate about crafting meaningful experiences through curiosity, empathy, and creativity. With a background in new media arts, I bring both an artist's sensibility and a designer's rationality to my work.

In 2023, I joined Chegg to design AI-powered experiences that enhance learing engagement and outcomes.

I earned my undergraduate degree in new media art at the University of California, San Diego, where I developed an artistic and human-centered perspective on human-computer interaction. To deepen my expertise in product design, I pursued a master’s degree in Interaction Design at the School of Visual Arts, graduating in 2023.

When I’m not designing, I enjoy making handicrafts, playing badminton, and capturing moments with my camera.

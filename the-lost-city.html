THE LOST CITY

Analogue Photography · 2018

Overview

I took a road trip with my friends to Salton City in California. Most residents have abandoned the place due to the severe pollution of Salton Sea, but there are still some people living there. The smell of dead fish was overwhelming once I got off my car. The place was full of traces of people who used to live there, like the worn-out office chair at the dock and the iron racks in the desert. We had a chat with a man whose garden was full of wine and beer bottles. Everything's gone with the salty wind.

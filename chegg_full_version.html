Context

Students are unaware of Course dashboard's value

Chegg's Course Dashboard provides various study tools to help students ace their courses in college. However, students only use Chegg to search for homework solutions and are unaware of the value of other study tools.

5%

Subscribers who use the Course Dashboard
are 5% less likely to request cancellation.

7.2%

However, only 7.2% of subscribers
visit the Course Dashboard.

Project brief

Increase repeat visits to course dashboard from homepage

👥 Target Users
Students who revisit the signed-in homepage after they subscribe to Chegg (not their first visit)

🎯 Scope
Design new modules on the signed-in homepage

⛳ User Need
Perceive the value of study tools available within their subscriptions

💼 Business Goal
Increase the visibility of various study tools on the Course Dashboard and drive repeat visits to the page

principles

Developing three design principles


Encouraging

Avoid using demotivating or stressful information

Personalized

Create personalized experience for each student

Student Centered

Incentivize students to do what they truly need

User Flow

Map out the new user flow

I created a new user flow for me and my team to understand how our project would affect and contribute to the entire user journey.

Placement

Where to place the design?

I tried out different placements with consideration of information hierarchy and the design in other ongoing projects. We started with the right rail but eventually placed the module in the central area below Pick Back Up because this would unleash its full potential.

ideation & wireframing

Choose three ideas out of five

I led a brainstorming session and generated five ideas with my teammates. I turned the ideas into sketches and wireframes. Based on feedback from the senior PMs and engineers, we decided to move on with the three most feasible and valuable ideas.

Final Output

Learning analysis

Happy Path

The Learning Analysis module provides a data-driven analysis of students' practice question performance. It suggests topics to focus on and drives students to study on the Course Dashboard.

Edge Cases & Empty States

I designed for rare but possible cases and empty states when there was data to show initially.
